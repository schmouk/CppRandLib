Detailed Description

This is the group for all the CppRandLib implementations of the Multiple Recursive pseudorandom Generators (MRG) algorithm.

Multiple Recursive Generators (MRGs) use recurrence to evaluate pseudo- random numbers suites. Recurrence is of the form:

x(i) = A * SUM[ x(i-k) ] mod M

for 2 to more k different values.

MRGs offer very large periods with the best known results in the evaluation of their randomness, as stated in the evaluation done by by Pierre L'Ecuyer and Richard Simard (Universite de Montreal) in "TestU01: A C Library for Empirical Testing of Random Number Generators - ACM Transactions on Mathematical Software, vol.33 n.4, pp.22-40, August 2007".
